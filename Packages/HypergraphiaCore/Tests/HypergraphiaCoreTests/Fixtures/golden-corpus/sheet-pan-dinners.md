# Sheet Pan Dinners

A short list of weeknight wins. The pattern is always the same: cut everything to similar size, toss with oil + acid + salt, roast at 425°F until things color.

## Sausage and peppers

Italian sausage links, bell peppers, red onion, fennel bulb, smashed garlic. Olive oil, splash of red wine vinegar, salt and pepper. 25 minutes, flip the sausage halfway. Crusty bread underneath catches the drippings — don't skip that part.

## Salmon and vegetables

Salmon fillets go on for the last 10 minutes only. Roast broccoli, cherry tomatoes, and lemon slices for 15 minutes first. Sesame oil, soy, ginger on the salmon. The lemon slices roast soft enough to eat — they're the secret.

## Chicken thighs and potatoes

Bone-in skin-on thighs, baby potatoes halved, rosemary, lemon. 40 minutes. The thigh fat renders into the potatoes, which is the whole point. Finish with parsley and flaky salt.

## Tofu and broccoli

Pressed extra-firm tofu in 1-inch cubes, tossed with cornstarch and oil for crispness. Broccoli florets. Roast 20 minutes, then drizzle with peanut sauce when it comes out. Lime wedges on the side.
