# Trip Ideas

Places I keep thinking about but haven't booked.

- Lisbon for a week, maybe two. The light, the food, friends who moved there.
- Driving the Pacific Coast Highway, slow, with the bike in the back of the truck.
- Tokyo in autumn — friends say spring is the cliché but autumn is better.
- A long weekend at that lake place we went to in 2019. Doing the same thing twice on purpose.
- Iceland's ring road in summer. Tent in the back of a 4x4.
- Five days in Mexico City. Eat at every place on the list.

Constraint: I never enjoy a trip I rushed onto the calendar. Two months of lead time is the floor; six months is better.
