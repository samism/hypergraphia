# Weeknight Pasta

The framework: aglio e olio is the platonic ideal. Garlic sliced thin, gently warmed in good olive oil, dried red pepper, parsley, lemon, parmesan. Pasta cooked one minute shy of al dente, finished in the pan with a ladle of starchy water. Total time: as long as the water takes to boil. Use this as the chassis and add: anchovy, peas, lemon zest, kale, a fried egg.
