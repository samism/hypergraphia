# Packing List

What goes in the carry-on, every trip.

Passport. Two changes of clothes max for a week-long trip — laundry exists everywhere. One pair of shoes you can walk fifteen miles in. Universal adapter. Anker battery and a single short cable for everything. Earplugs. Eye mask. A book you actually want to read, not the book you think you should be reading. Painkillers and a small bottle of melatonin. A copy of the passport in a different bag.

What I stopped packing: extra "just in case" outfits, a second pair of shoes, the second book, anything I tell myself is "for emergencies."
