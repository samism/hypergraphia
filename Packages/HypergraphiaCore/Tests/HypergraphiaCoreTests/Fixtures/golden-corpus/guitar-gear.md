# Guitar Gear

What I actually use, after years of tweaking.

## The guitars

A Telecaster (American Standard, ash body, maple neck) and a hollowbody (Gibson ES-335 reissue). The Telecaster is for everything from country to indie rock; the 335 is for jazz comping and warmer rhythm tones. I had a Stratocaster for a long time and sold it — the Telecaster covered the same ground for me, and I never reached for the Strat.

## Amps

A Fender Princeton Reverb '65 reissue (15W tube) for everything that needs to be loud at low volumes — the speaker breakup at 4 on the dial is the sound. A Quilter Aviator (solid state, 50W) for gigs where I need clean headroom. The Princeton is what I'd pick if I had to pick one.

## Pedals

Boss TU-3 tuner. Strymon Flint (reverb + tremolo). JHS Morning Glory (transparent overdrive). Earthquaker Devices Avalanche Run (delay). That's the whole board. I cycled through dozens of fuzz and modulation pedals over the years; nothing earned a permanent spot besides those four.

## Strings

D'Addario NYXL 10-46 on the Tele, 11-50 flatwounds on the 335. Flatwounds for jazz feel right and last forever; they're more expensive but the cost-per-month is lower.
