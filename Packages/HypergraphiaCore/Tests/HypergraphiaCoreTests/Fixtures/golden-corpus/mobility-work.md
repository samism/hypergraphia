# Mobility Work

Five-minute routine I do most mornings before coffee.

## The sequence

Cat-cow on the floor for ten reps. World's-greatest-stretch each side for thirty seconds. Couch stretch for hip flexors, sixty seconds per side. Shoulder dislocates with a band, ten reps. Wrist circles and prayer stretch.

## Why bother

If I skip mobility for two weeks I notice it on every other lift. The kettlebell rack position requires thoracic rotation; squats require ankle dorsiflexion; pressing requires shoulder external rotation. The five minutes pays back compound interest across every session.
